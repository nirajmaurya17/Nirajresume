Academic Projects

1) Remote Control Home Appliances using RF Module (2015)

The aim of developing this project was to regulate the voltage and control home appliances with a remote by using the RF module. This concept was more reliable and has wider range as compared to an infrared model.

2) Contra-rotation Wind Power Generation (2016)

Developed a windmill with the blades on both sides, front and rear, it has a rotor and stator, both as a rotating part. Due to this we get a double output as compared to an ordinary windmill.

3) Pressure converted into electricity (2017)

Developed a series parallel combination of piezoelectric plates, so that when pressure is applied to the plates, as a result, we get voltage as an output.

4) Development of Delay Trip Mechanism in any electrical equipment (2017-2018)

The aim of developing this project is to avoid nuisance tripping of ACBs (Air Circuit Breakers) in an under-voltage situation.
